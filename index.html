FAQ

Is Heartbeat free?

Yes.

Will Heartbeat be ported to Linux or Windows?

No. Being myself a Mac user, I will focus only on MacOS to provide the best experience possible and a correct support.

How can I extend Heartbeat?

You can start developing your own plugin by reading the documentation. If you are not a developer, you can send me a request.

Are you collecting personal data?

No, not personal data. However, I do log errors that you may encounter and some metrics to understand how you use Heartbeat.
